# Projects and Games References

## Marooned
FPS boomer shooter, has nice FPS camera and 3D weapons, old school Doom style billboards and dungeon design
 - Repo: https://github.com/Jhyde927/Marooned
 - Details: [marooned.md](marooned.md)

## r3d
Raylib based game engine/full renderer, has basic physics, lots of shader examples, quite fleshed out with tons of examples
 - Repo: https://github.com/Bigfoot71/r3d
 - Details: [r3d.md](r3d.md)

## Kinemium-Engine
Full Luau game engine on Zune runtime. Opposite to our philosophy but has a few stealable ideas (FFI binding generator, octree, pure-scripting-language physics)
 - Repo: https://github.com/Kinemium/Kinemium-Engine
 - Details: [kinemium-engine.md](kinemium-engine.md)
